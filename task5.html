Cinnamon Rolls

ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yest,sugar
  2. melt the butter and add themixture
  3. Add the flour and the mix untill combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl
